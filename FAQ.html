Frequently Asked Questions

1. How can I book a ticket?

Simply enter your starting location, destination, and travel date, then select a bus and proceed to payment.

2. Is there a limit on the number of tickets I can book?

No, you can book multiple tickets, but ensure the seats are available for the chosen bus and date.

3. What payment methods are accepted?

We accept various payment methods, including credit/debit cards, mobile wallets, and online banking.

4. Can I cancel my ticket after booking?

Yes, you can cancel your ticket. Refund policies may apply, depending on the time of cancellation.

5. How can I check the status of my booking?

You can view your booking status by logging into your account and visiting the "My Bookings" section.

6. How do I modify my booking?

You can modify your booking details through the "My Bookings" section in your account before the travel date.
